-------------------------------
Trong index.html, tim dong:

    <img src="AVATAR_URL_HERE" alt="avatar" onerror="...">

Cach lay link avatar Discord:
  - Mo Discord tren web/app.
  - Vao trang ca nhan cua ban -> bam vao anh dai dien -> chon "Copy Avatar URL"
    (hoac chuot phai vao avatar trong chat -> Copy Image Address).

  - Dan link do thay vao cho AVATAR_URL_HERE.

Neu khong dung link online, ban co the:
  - Tai anh avatar ve, dat ten la avatar.png, bo vao chung thu muc voi index.html.
  - Sua src="AVATAR_URL_HERE" thanh src="avatar.png".

Neu link loi hoac khong tai duoc, chu "忍" se tu hien ra thay the (khong bi vo giao dien).


2) SETUP DECORATION (avatar decoration cua Discord) https://cdn.discordapp.com/media/v1/collectibles-shop/1228251144065777765/animated
----------------------------------------------------
Discord decoration la file PNG (thuong co nen trong suot) phu vien quanh avatar.

Trong index.html, tim dong:

    <img class="avatar-decor" src="DECOR_URL_HERE" alt="decoration" ...>

Cach lay link decoration:
  - Vao Discord Settings (User Settings) -> Profiles -> Avatar Decoration.
  - Chon decoration ban muon dung (hoac decoration dang active).
  - Discord decoration co dang URL:
      https://cdn.discordapp.com/avatar-decoration-presets/<asset-id>.png
    Ban co the lay link nay bang cach:
      + Dung Discord API (GET /users/@me) neu ban co bot/token rieng, xem field

      + Hoac don gian hon: tai file PNG decoration tu cac trang chia se asset
        Discord decoration (tim theo ten decoration), luu ve may.
  - Neu tai ve file rieng, dat ten decor.png, bo cung thu muc voi index.html,
    roi sua src="DECOR_URL_HERE" thanh src="decor.png".

Neu khong co decoration, xoa han dong <img class="avatar-decor" ...> trong
phan <div class="avatar-wrap"> de tranh loi anh vo hinh chiem cho.
